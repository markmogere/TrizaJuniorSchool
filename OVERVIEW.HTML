Admission Overview Guide

Step 1: Registration

Visit the school to fill out an application form.

Step 2: Assessment

The admissions department will schedule an assessment in English and Mathematics in additional to understanding your child's unique needs. Assessment fee per child is Ksh 500.

Step 3: Information Capture

After a successful assessment, an admission form is filled out offering the student a placement in the school.

Step 4: Acceptance Fee

An ACCEPTANCE FEE and CAUTION MONEY of Ksh 3,000 and Ksh 500 respectively is paid by the parent to secure a place for their child. The ACCEPTANCE FEE will be forwarded as an initial school fee deposit.

Step 5: School Fee Deposit

Full school fees and caution fee paid in before ennrollment.

Step 5: Enjoy Learning

Your child is successfully enrolled in our institution
