School Fee Structure

PLEASE NOTE

Fees are reviewed every 2 years.
The admission fee is payable for students enrolling for the first time to secure a place for the child in the institution.
The caution fee is payable ONCE upon admission and is refundable after deduction of dues if any after the student leaves the institution.
Each learner MUST participate in ONE competitive sport and Club each term.
Meals cost Ksh 8,000 per child, per term.
The following school items are available in the school:- SPORTS T-SHIRT @ Ksh 600
SWEATER @ Ksh 800
TIE @ Ksh 200
ASSESSMENT BOOK @ Ksh 300
DIARY @ Ksh 200
TRACK SUIT @ Ksh 2,500
*PAID AT THE SCHOOL AND RECEIPT ISSUED



WHAT IS NOT INCLUDED IN THE SCHOOL FEE?

Kindly note that the following are not included in the school fees;
  • All stationery.

  • Class trip expenses.

  • Entrance fees for external sporting competitions.

  • School and sports uniforms.
