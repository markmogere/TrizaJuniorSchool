Guidance and Counseling

At Triza Junior School, our guidance and counseling sessions, facilitated by Ms. Sarah Johnson, our licensed counselor, are fundamental components of our students' growth.
Every Wednesday, Ms. Johnson provides a secure space where students can openly address concerns, anxieties, or personal matters impacting their academic performance or social lives.
Beyond dedicated sessions, our counseling program is seamlessly integrated into the curriculum through life skills lessons, enriching students' learning experiences.
Ms. Johnson's expertise in student well-being, stress management, and academic support enables her to assist students in navigating challenges and devising strategies for personal and academic achievements.
Rest assured, all counseling sessions prioritize confidentiality, ensuring students' privacy, unless there is a potential risk of harm to themselves or others.
